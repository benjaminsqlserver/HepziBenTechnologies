Home  /  About

A Nigerian technology firm built on craftsmanship, honesty, learning and partnership.

Hepziben Technologies Ltd is incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act, 2020. We specialise in software development, IT consulting, systems integration and capacity building for organisations across the private, public and educational sectors.

Brand story

The name is a promise.

Hepziben (Hebrew Hephzibah) means "my delight is in her." It reflects our founding belief that the technology we build should be a source of delight, not friction, for the people who use it.

We exist to engineer software that empowers organisations and the people inside them to do their best work. We treat code, design, and documentation as professional work worth doing well.

Mission

To engineer reliable, well-designed software and digital infrastructure that helps Nigerian and international organisations operate more effectively.

Vision

To be recognised as one of West Africa's most trusted engineering-led technology firms by 2035.

Our values

Four commitments that shape every engagement.

Craftsmanship

We treat code, design, and documentation as professional work worth doing well. Cutting corners is borrowed time we never wanted to owe.

Honesty

We say what we can do, do what we said, and tell the truth when something goes wrong. No buzzwords. No hand-waving. No surprises hidden in invoices.

Learning

We invest in growing ourselves, our team, and the wider Nigerian tech community — through tutorials, open source, and active mentoring.

Partnership

Our clients are partners in shared outcomes, not transactions on a ledger. We measure success by the durability of what we leave behind.
